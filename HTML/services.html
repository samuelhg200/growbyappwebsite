Full Mobile-App Development & Launch Process

At GrowByApp we manage the whole development process for you.

  • Pre-design

    Tools: Lucid Chart, Trello, etc.

    Description: During The pre-desing stage the requirements for the app will be agreed upon and a product backlog will be produced. This does not limit your ability to introduce further changes during the rest of the development stages.

  • Design

    Technologies/Tools:Adobe Photoshop, AdobeXd, Lucid Chart, etc.

    Description: During this stage UI and UX design of your app is produced and iterated upon until a satisfactory blueprint is produced to start development. We aim to design an app that clearly reflects your brand image, as well as providing the best attainable user experience.

  • Development - Android & IOS

    Technologies/Tools: React Native, Firebase, Github, Android Studio, Visual Studio Code, etc.

    Description: At GrowByApp we have decided to take a Hybrid approach to Mobile App Development by using React Native as our technology of choice. This approach allows us to offer you the best quality-to-price ratio, as the same codebase can be used to develop both the IOS & Android app, hence halving development & support - time and cost.

    This a modern approach, widely used accross the industry. With huge companies such as Facebook, Instagram and Walmart using it in their apps today. React Native provides a reliable and efficient solution with astounding performance and capabilities.

  • Testing

    Technologies/Tools: RNTL, Selenium, Manual User Testing, etc.

    Description: Testing is sometimes overlooked, however at GrowByApp we understand its importance, as it is key to providing the users with the best possible bug-free experience. Our solution consists in testing the Android and IOS app versions independently of each others, using a mix of manual and automated testing during all, and in parallel with, the development stage. Documentation of the different test cases conducted is then delivered when the app has been assessed and proven to a satisfactory degree.

  • Deployment to App Store & Goole Play Store

    Description: Deploying your app to the App store and Google Play Store can be annoying, and at times distressing. We do that for you, creating a developer account under your name of choice for you to access on each app marketplace and publishing the apps with everything that this entails.

  • Marketing

    Tools: Final Cut Pro X, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe After Effects, etc.

    Description: Marketing your app appropriately is extremely important. Creating quality promotional content will impact your users' perception of your app and your business. At GrowByApp we offer a solution that works, by creating promotional content tailored to your users and customized to match your brand. We will also build the groundwork for your social media presence, if not yet established. This promotional content, will cover a demo of the app, multiple professionally edited videos and content to promote it and much more.

  • Long-term Support

    Technologies/Tools: Firebase, React Native, etc.

    Description: Mobile apps tend to get out of date soon if not properly maintained. We offer support during a mutually agreed period of time - so you don't have to worry about your app becoming obsolete in an ever changing mobile app marketplace. An adequate app maintainance is key to deliver the best possible user experience.

    Our Support service can cover the following:
    -Keeping the app up to date with OS and hardware changes.
    -Server & DB maintanance.
    -Performance monitoring.
    -Adding new features.
    -And others... (on request)
